Lessons & Mistakes — May

⚠️
XAUUSD May 7 · −$56.67 across 3 trades. Stop on Trade #1 was hit cleanly — that should have ended the session. Instead re-entered the same broken zone 3 times in 6 minutes. Trades #3 and #4 were entered within 1 second of each other. Fix: When stop is hit, close the chart for 30 minutes minimum. The setup is dead for the session.
📝
SPY Puts May 7 · Captured only 25% of move. Setup was correct (algo + fib + H&S confluence) but exited at +$41 when the move gave $150–200. Fear of profit give-back overrode the system. Fix: Define target before entry. Hold to target unless system signals flip — not because it "feels" like time to exit.
✅
XAUUSD #1 was a good trade that lost. Stop placed at 4712, hit with normal slippage to 4711.80, exited correctly. Plan followed. This is exactly how a planned loss is supposed to look. The mistake was everything that came after.
